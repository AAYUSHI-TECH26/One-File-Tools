A print color-management studio that lives in your browser. Convert screen color into press-accurate CMYK, soft-proof against SWOP, FOGRA, GRACoL and Japan Color, and catch gamut clipping before it costs you a print run.

Everything you'd normally open Illustrator for.

Built for the moment you just need to know: will this exact color survive being printed?

◐

Real colorimetry

Full sRGB → linear → XYZ(D65) → Bradford-adapted XYZ(D50) → Lab / LCh / OKLab / OKLCH pipeline — the same space ICC workflows reason in.

▦

Iterative CMYK solve

Instead of inverting RGB, the engine iteratively searches CMYK combinations to minimize ΔE2000 against your target, respecting each profile's TAC and GCR curve.

◧

Soft-proof slider

Side-by-side and split-slider proofing simulates paper white, dot gain, and black point compensation for coated and uncoated stocks.

✦

Rendering intents

Switch between Perceptual, Relative Colorimetric, Absolute Colorimetric and Saturation and watch gamut-mapping behavior change instantly.

◈

Gamut & ΔE visualization

Lab a*b* scatter with sRGB vs. press-gamut boundaries, hue wheel, lightness graph, and out-of-gamut clip flags.

▤

Production tooling

Rich black recipes, TAC warnings, neutral gray checking, ink cost estimation, and export to CSS, JSON, ASE, and GPL.
